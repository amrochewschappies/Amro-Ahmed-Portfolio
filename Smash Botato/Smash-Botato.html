Project Breakdown

"Smash Botato" is a fun and fast-paced game where players mash potatoes as they move across the screen, while also avoiding the occasional bomb that appears. With its simple yet engaging mechanics, the game challenges players to maintain focus and rhythm as the intensity ramps up.

Developed as part of a game design project, Smash Botato explores the psychological effects of music on player performance. The game features two iterations, each paired with a distinct background soundtrack. One iteration uses tension-eliciting music to create a sense of suspense, while the other incorporates energetic music to boost motivation and engagement. This dual-mode gameplay allows players to experience firsthand how music can influence their reaction times and decision-making under pressure.

With its blend of lighthearted visuals and intriguing research-based design, Smash Botato serves as both a delightful gaming experience and an insightful exploration of the interplay between music and player psychology.
