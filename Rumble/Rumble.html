Project Breakdown

"Rumble" is a fast-paced, competitive local multiplayer game where players go head-to-head in a battle for the crown. The objective is simple: manipulate the height of tiles by lowering and raising them strategically to build a path that leads to the crown, which is floating above the map. The first player to reach it wins the round!

The game features a variety of dynamic and interactive maps that challenge players to think on their feet and outmaneuver their opponent. Players can raise or lower tiles beneath their feet, creating obstacles or bridges for themselves and their rival. Timing and precision are key, as every move can either help you advance toward the crown or block your opponentâ€™s progress.

Developed with a focus on local multiplayer fun, "Rumble" brings friends together for exciting, head-to-head action, testing both reaction times and strategic planning. Its combination of fast-paced gameplay and simple yet challenging mechanics ensures that no two rounds are ever the same, making it a perfect game for casual, competitive fun.
