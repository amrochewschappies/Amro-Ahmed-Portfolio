Project Breakdown

"Hide & See-bu" is an inventive take on the classic prop hunt genre, designed to test players' observation, memory, and strategic thinking. The game begins with a 30-second preparation phase where all players can freely explore and analyze the map. During this time, the hider gets a chance to memorize potential hiding spots and objects in the environment, setting the stage for creative and convincing disguises.

When the hiding phase begins, the hider selects a prop to blend seamlessly into the surroundings. Meanwhile, the seeker prepares to locate the hider using sharp observational skills and a keen memory of the mapâ€™s layout. Each round is a battle of wits, with the hider relying on stealth and creativity while the seeker employs deduction and strategy to win.

With its emphasis on adaptability and psychological gameplay, Hide & See-bu offers players a highly engaging experience that evolves with every match. Its dynamic blend of hide-and-seek mechanics and player-driven creativity ensures fun for all, making it a standout addition to any multiplayer game library.
