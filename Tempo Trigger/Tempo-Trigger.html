Project Breakdown

This rhythm-based FPS challenges players to synchronize their actions with a driving musical beat. Inspired by titles like BPM: Bullets Per Minute and DOOM Eternal, the game combines intense, fast-paced combat with rhythm mechanics, creating a unique experience where every shot, dash, and grenade throw must be perfectly timed. The result is a seamless fusion of gameplay and soundtrack, ensuring that each encounter feels dynamic and rewarding.

The game focuses on delivering a polished single-level experience, featuring handcrafted environments, meticulously designed enemy patterns, and a climactic boss battle. Players utilize a variety of weapons, consumables, and abilities to take down rhythmically synced enemies while adapting to an ever-evolving battlefield. The progression system encourages strategic decision-making as players manage their health, ammo, and upgrades in the heat of battle.

With its vibrant visuals, immersive sound design, and tightly integrated rhythm mechanics, the game immerses players in a pulse-pounding journey where every beat matters. This project exemplifies a dedication to creative game design, blending music, movement, and mechanics into an exhilarating experience.
